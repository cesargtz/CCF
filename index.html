We are a family owned furniture manufacturing business that was founded in 2003. We're dedicated to providing high quality and service our customers deserve.

In our gallery, you'll find different styles and designs, with rustic furniture being our specialty. We use ponderosa pine and red cedar upon request. Because there is no middle man, we can offer high quality furniture at affordable prices.

We offer various different styles including
  • log Furniture
  • Western
  • Vintage
  • Farmhouse
  • Mexican Traditional

We manufacture an extensive line of bedrooms, buffet tables, dining sets, coffee tables, and benches. Our most popular items include log-made furniture for cabins.
